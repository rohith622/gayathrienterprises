Fertilizers - Gayathri Enterprises

Fertilizers play a vital role in modern agriculture by providing essential nutrients that plants need for healthy growth and high yields. They enrich the soil with important elements like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, which are often lost after repeated cropping. Fertilizers help improve plant strength, enhance root development, and increase resistance to diseases and pests. By maintaining soil fertility, they ensure that crops receive a balanced supply of nutrients throughout their growth period. The proper and timely use of fertilizers helps farmers achieve better-quality produce and higher productivity, contributing significantly to food security and agricultural sustainability. Fertilizers are broadly classified into two types â€” organic and inorganic (chemical) fertilizers. Organic fertilizers such as compost, manure, and green manure improve soil texture and promote long-term fertility. Inorganic fertilizers like Urea, DAP (Di-Ammonium Phosphate), and MOP (Muriate of Potash) provide specific nutrients quickly and effectively. Using a balanced combination of both types helps maintain soil health and maximize crop output.
